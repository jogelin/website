## **\[Core\] Activate/Deactivate Project Crystal**

Until now you could use the environment variable [NX\_ADD\_PLUGINS](https://nx.dev/reference/environment-variables#environment-variables) to activate or deactivate the inferred targets. A new property `useInferencePlugins: false` was added to the `nx.json` to do the same:

[![](/blog/images/ad64c0d2-11f5-41b3-908a-1a0664c10e25.png)](https://nx.dev/nx-api/devkit/documents/NxJsonConfiguration?source=post_page-----067889301e55--------------------------------#useinferenceplugins)
